Interview Database schema:

Submitter
	SubmitterID (PK)
	Name
	Email 
	AddressID (FK)


Address
	AddressID (PK)
	Street
	City
	State
	Zip


1 to Many relationship (1 Submitter - Many Permit Applications)

PermitApplication
	PermitID (PK)
	SubmitterID (FK)
	PermitType
	County
	DateSubmitted



Sql example:
-- Find Permits within a date range, grouped by submitter city

DECLARE @StartDate DATE = '2024-01-01';
DECLARE @EndDate DATE   = '2024-12-31';

SELECT 
    a.City,
    COUNT(pa.PermitId) AS TotalPermits
FROM PermitApplication pa
JOIN Submitter s ON pa.SubmitterId = s.SubmitterId
JOIN Address a ON s.AddressId = a.AddressId
WHERE pa.DateSubmitted BETWEEN @StartDate AND @EndDate
GROUP BY a.City
ORDER BY a.City;
